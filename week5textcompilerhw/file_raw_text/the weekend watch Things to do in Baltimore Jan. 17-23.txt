Honor a civil rights icon, see monster trucks soar through the air, learn how art is created, enjoy a laugh-filled play or attend a local literary giant's birthday party.
- John Coffren
Monday: MLK Dare to Dream Day
Help honor Civil Rights icon Martin Luther King Jr. and his enduring legacy at MLK Dare to Dream Day at the American Visionary Art Museum, 800 Key Hwy.  Activities include an art workshop, open mic and poetry slam, artist talk, traditional African music and dance, and more. The event is free. RSVP at avam.org/event-details/free-mlk-dare-to-dream-day
Monday 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Ongoing: Monster Jam 2025
Watch super trucks soar with oversized tires spinning and mud flying at Monster Jam at the CFG Bank Arena, 201 W. Baltimore St. Featured trucks and drivers include Grave Digger (Krysten Anderson), El Toro Loco (MJ Solorio), Megalodon (Mikayla Tulachka) and more. Ticket prices start at $35. Dates and times are Friday at 7 p.m., Saturday at 1 p.m. and 7 p.m. and Sunday at 1 p.m. monsterjam.com/en-us/events/baltimore-md/jan-17-2025-jan-19-2025/
Friday-Sunday
Ongoing: 'Art and Process'
Learn about the creative process at "Art and Process: Drawings, Paintings and Sculptures" from the 19th Century Collection at the Walters Art Museum, 600 N. Charles St. Drawings and sketches record the choices made on 60 works of art. Days and hours are as follows: Wednesday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. and Thursday 1 p.m. to 8 p.m. Admission is free. thewalters.org/exhibitions/art-process/ 
Through March 9
Ongoing: Room Service
Gordon Miller must outwit antagonists from a hotel executive to a collection agency rep to ensure his Broadway show goes on in the classic farce Room Service, performed at the Vagabond Players, 806 S. Broadway.  Ticket prices range from $25 to $28. Dates and showtimes this weekend are: Friday and Saturday at 8 p.m. and Sunday at 2 p.m.  vagabondplayers.org/show/155/room-service
Through Feb. 2
Saturday: Edgar Allan Poe Birthday & Poe Toaster Murder Mystery
Celebrate the birthday of a literary giant and solve a crime at the Edgar Allan Poe Birthday & Poe Toaster Murder Mystery at Westminster Hall and Burying Ground, 519 W. Fayette St. Discover who murdered the Poe Toaster, enjoy party refreshments,  guided tour and graveside birthday toast. Tickets cost $39. poeinbaltimore.org/event/edgar-allan-poe-birthday-poe-toaster-murder-mystery-2025/
Saturday 6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.
